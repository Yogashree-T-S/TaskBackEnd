1. write a MongoDB query to display all the restaurant which is  in borough Bronx. 
2. write a MongoDB query to find the restaurants that achieved a score, more than 80 but less than 100
3. Write MongoDB query to find the restaurants that do not prepare any cuisine of "American" and their grade score more than 70 and latitude less than -65.754168.

________________________________________1_______________________________________________
db.Food.find({borough:"Bronx"})

________________________________________2_______________________________________________

db.Food.find({$and:[{"grades.score":{$gt:80}},{"grades.score":{$lt:100}}]})

________________________________________3_______________________________________________

db.Food.find({$and : [ {"cuisine":{$ne:"American "}} ,{$and : [ {"grades.score":{$gt:70}}, {"address.coord.0":{$lt:-65.754168}}] }  ] })

_______________________________________or3_______________________________________________

db.Food.find({$and : [ {"cuisine":{$ne:"American "}} , {"grades.score":{$gt:70}} , {"address.coord.0":{$lt:-65.754168}}]})
